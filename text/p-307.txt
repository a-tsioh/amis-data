something burning 噼啪聲，爆裂聲
talacay - 1. pineapple 鳳梨 2. a wild plant with minature

talacowa - 1. Where are (you) going? (你) 到那裡去? 2.
although 雖然
Talacowa kiso? - Where are you going? 你要去那裡?
Talacowa o mafana'ay cigra nikawrira awaay ko taneg nira. -
Although he knows, he has no wisdom.　雖然他是知識份子但是
沒有才能
talagawan / tagarawan. rooster＊male chicken 公雞
talahkal - 1. to come to shore ＊ to come to the surface 上岸，浮
上水面 2. to be revealed 揭露 3. to appear 出版，公開
Talahkalto anini ko tatiihay a dmak nira. - The wrong he did has
come to light. 他的壞事以暴露出來了，他的劣跡已東窗事發了
O pipatalahkal to dmak iso ko ga'ay. - You should confess the
truth about what you did. 你還是坦白承認你所做的一切為上策
Talahkalto ko fotig. - The fish came to shore. 魚衝上沙灘

將顯現
