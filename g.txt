# 單字與單字間請空一行
ga'
connective particle meaning then * as a result
連接詞，好讓，以便

ga'ay 
okay * agreed * acceptable
好，同意，贊成，可接受
good * fine
好，很好，可
    O ga'ayay a romi'ad anini. 
    This is a good day (refers to weather or opportunity).
    今天是好日子，今天是好天氣
    Maga'ay kako a tayra haw? Ga'ayto. 
    May I go? Fine.
    我可以去嗎?可以
    Maga'ay ko adada nira. 
    His illness is gone.
    他的病好了
    Awaay ko naga'ayan. 
    There is no improvement.
    沒有起色
    Saga'ay sa ko harateg ako anini.
    I feel much better (about it) now. (implied that I had been upset)
    今天我很安心,我的心情好多了(煩亂，吵架，發脾氣之後)
    O kaga'ayan ako konini. 
    This is my opportunity.
    這是我的好機會
    O ga'ayay a tamdaw cigra. 
    He is a good man.
    他是好人
